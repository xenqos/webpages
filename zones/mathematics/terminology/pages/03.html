Mathematics  •  Terminology

Arithmetics

$=$equality$5 = 2+3$
$\ne$inequality$5 \ne 4$
$>$greater than$5 > 4$
$<$less than$4 < 5$
$\ge$greater than or equal to$5 \ge 4$
$\le$less than or equal to$4 \le 5$
$( \ )$parentheses precedence$2 \, ( 3 + 5 ) = 16$
$[ \ ]$brackets precedence$[ ( 1 + 2 ) \, ( 1 + 5 ) ] = 18$
$+$addition$1 + 1 = 2$
$-$subtraction$2 - 1 = 1$
$\pm$both + and - operations$3 \pm 5 = 8 \ and \ -2$
$\mp$both - and + operations$3 \mp 5 = -2 \ and \ 8$
$\ast$multiplication$2 * 3 = 6$
$\times$multiplication$2 \times 3 = 6$
$\cdot$multiplication dot$2 \cdot 3 = 6$
$\div $division sign$6 \div 2 = 3$
$/$division slash$6 / 2 = 3$
$-$division fraction$\frac{6}{2} = 3$
$\mathrm{mod}$modulus, remainder $7 \pmod{2} = 1$
$.$decimal separator$2.56 = 2+56/100$
$a{}^{b}$ power exponent $2{}^{3} = 8$
$a \ \hat{} \ b$ caret exponent $2 \ \hat{} \ 3 = 8$
$\sqrt{a}$square root$\sqrt{9} = \pm 3$
$\sqrt[3]{a}$cube root$\sqrt[3]{8} = \pm 2 $
$\sqrt[4]{a}$fourth root$\sqrt[4]{16} = \pm 2 $
$\sqrt[n]{a}$n-th root (radical)$for \ n = 3 \ \sqrt[n]{8} = \pm 2 $
$\%$percent$10 \% \times 30 = 3$
$\text{‰}$per-mille$10\text{‰} \times 30 = 0.3$
$ppm$per-million$10ppm \times 30=0.0003$
$ppb$ per-billion $10ppb \times 30=3 \times 10^{7}$
$ppt$ per-trillion $10ppt \times 30=3 \times 10^{10}$
