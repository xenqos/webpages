** Part A: ** Pivot the left foot 45 degrees to the left, and shift your weight forward as you make a scooping motion with both arms in front of your left knee.

** Part B: ** Continue the scooping motion until your arms are crossed at the wrist, palms facing inward (left hand inside). Stand up on your left leg with your hands crossed in front of your chest and raise your right knee.

** Part C: ** Kick with the right leg and open the arms as in other kicks. (Figure 45.1)
