Game

Basic Strategy

Rule 1

Play hands according to WinRate.

Rule 2

Play Flop only if it improved the hand.

Rule 3

Play a drawing hand only if after Flop the hand has four cards.

Rule 4

Play a top pair with a big kicker or overpair after Flop.

Rule 5

Reassess the board after each round.

Rule 6

Raise with the best hand. Do not limp.

Rule 7

Fold when it is obvious. Minimize losses.

Rule 8

Consider the table position.
