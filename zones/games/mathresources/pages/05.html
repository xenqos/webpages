Menthal Rules

7,8,9 - Addition

Formulas:
$x+7=x+10-3$
$x+8=x+10-2$
$x+9=x+10-1$

Examples:
$56+7=56+10-3=63$
$47+8=47+10-2=55$
$73+9=73+10-1=82$

7,8,9 - Subtraction

Formulas:
$x-7=x-10+3$
$x-8=x-10+2$
$x-9=x-10+1$

Examples:
$46-7=46-10+3=39$
$57-8=57-10+2=49$
$88-9=88+10-1=79$

5 - Multiplication

Formulas:
$x×5 = x÷2×10$

Example:
$88×5=88÷2×10=440$

9 - Multiplication

Formulas:
$x × 9 = x × 10 − x$

Examples:
$13 × 9 = 13 × 10 − 13 = 117$

Addition of Two-Digit Numbers

The Last Digit >= 5

round up 5
perform addition
subtract the increase

54+39=54+40-1=93
26+38=26+40-2=64

The Last Digit < 5

adding by place value
first tens, then units

57+32=57+30+2=89

Addition of Three-Digit Numbers

break down three-digit numbers
into hundreds, tens, units
add them one by one

249+533=(200+500)+(40+30)+(9+3)=782
