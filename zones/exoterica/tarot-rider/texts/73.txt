# Nine of Pentacles
^[72.Nine-of-Pentacles.webp]

### A.E.W.

#### Upright
Prudence, safety, success, accomplishment, certitude, discernment.

%[l|Additionally]
Prompt fulfilment of what is presaged by neighbouring cards.

%[l|Recurrence]
2 - Receipt.
3 - Success.
4 - A good friend.

#### Reversed
Roguery, deception, voided project, bad faith.

%[l|Additionally]
Vain hopes.

%[l|Recurrence]
2 - A small profit.
3 - Imprudence.
4 - Usury.

### S.L.M.M.

#### Upright
Discretion, Circumspection, Prudence, Discernment

#### Reversed
Deceit, Bad faith, Artifices, Deception.

### Description
A woman, with a bird upon her wrist, stands amidst a great abundance of grapevines in the garden of a manorial house. It is a wide domain, suggesting plenty in all things. Possibly it is her own possession and testifies to material well-being.
