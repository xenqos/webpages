34 step punch down

advance step, plant punch
шаг удар вниз
шаг вперед, удар вниз


Step straight ahead with your left foot, keeping your weight on your right. As you shift your weight forward and square your hips, bring your right arm forward, make a fist, and punch forward and down. At the same time, push your left hand palm down past the left knee. (Figure 42.1)
