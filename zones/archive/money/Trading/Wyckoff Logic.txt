## Accumulation Events

^[.wyckoff_accumulation_01.webp]

### 1 - Support
Buying begins to provide support after markdown.
Volume increases and price spread widens, signaling that the markdown is ending.

### 2 - Climax
Selling panic is absorbed and at bottom.

### 3 - Rally
Buying. The high defines the upper boundary of an accumulation TR.

### 4 - Test
Test SC level. Volume should be low at support in the area of the SC. ST can be multiple.

### 5 - Test
Test for supply. Test makes a higher low on lesser volume.

### 6 - Test
Piercing test.

### 7 - Last Point of Support
Diminished spread and volume. Can be multiple.

### 8 - Back Up
Profit-taking and Test for additional supply around resistance. Precedes Range End.

### 9 - Sign of Strength
Takes place after the last test.
